Jalebi

Jalebi, a beloved Indian sweet, is a crispy, golden-brown spiral delight soaked in sweet syrup, often enjoyed as a celebratory treat.


Caramel Flan

Caramel flan is a decadent dessert featuring a silky smooth custard base topped with a rich, caramelized sugar layer.


Rasgulla

rasgulla is a spongy, sweet dumpling made with chhena, an Indian cottage cheese, soaked in a light syrup.


Gajar Halwa

Gajar halwa is a rich and indulgent Indian dessert made with grated carrots, milk, sugar, and aromatic spices like cardamom and saffron.


Gardenias

Gardenias are elegant flowers known for their large, waxy white blooms and intoxicatingly sweet fragrance.


Lotus

The lotus, a symbol of purity and enlightenment, is a beautiful aquatic flower with large, fragrant blooms that rise above the water's surface.


Campanula

Campanula flowers are delicate, bell-shaped blooms that add a touch of elegance to gardens with their vibrant colors and graceful form.


Sunflower

The sunflower, a symbol of happiness and optimism, is a tall, vibrant flower known for its large, sunny yellow blooms that follow the sun's path across the sky.


Kajukatli

Kaju katli is a rich, melt-in-your-mouth Indian sweet made with cashew nuts, sugar, and cardamom.


Motichur Laddu

Motichur laddu is a popular Indian sweet made with small, crispy boondi balls dipped in a sweet syrup and shaped into round laddu.


Kesar Peda

Kesar peda is a rich and creamy Indian sweet made with milk, sugar, and saffron, known for its delicate texture and rich flavor.


Sonpapdi

Sonpapdi is a crunchy, flaky Indian sweet made with semolina, ghee, and sugar.


Rose

The rose, a symbol of love and beauty, is a flowering plant with fragrant blooms in various colors like red, pink, yellow, and white.


Arum

The Arum lily is a striking flower with a unique, trumpet-shaped bloom and large, glossy leaves.


Daisies

Daisies are cheerful, simple flowers with bright yellow centers surrounded by delicate white petals.


Frangipani

The Frangipani is a tropical flower known for its beautiful, fragrant blooms in various colors like white, yellow, and pink.
